EduYear is Mainly Inspired from Instagram, twitter and Gradup. Key Points about the app are:

  • Similar to Instagram, we have here the follow and following feature available here with a verified mark too.
    • Any special event organised by the company (EduYear) can be displayed in the notification section in banner form.
  • Personal User Dashboard where user can add/edit their profile pic, bio and view their uploaded posts.
